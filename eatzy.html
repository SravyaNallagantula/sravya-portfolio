Overview

Eatzy is a modern food delivery application designed to connect users with local eateries efficiently and securely.

Problem

Users struggled to find reliable, local food options with a user-friendly digital experience.

Solution

Designed an intuitive app for browsing restaurants, tracking orders in real time, and offering secure payment options.
